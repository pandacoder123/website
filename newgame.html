Shellshockers

ShellShockers is a first-person shooter game played in the browser, it is also known as Egg Battle Royale.

Doge Miner

Doge Miner is a popular incremental game in which players mine Dogecoins by clicking on a button and then use the coins to upgrade their mining equipment and other items to mine even more Dogecoins.

Smashkarts

SmashKarts is a multiplayer kart racing game where players compete against each other in fast-paced and action-packed races, using power-ups and special abilities to gain an advantage over their opponents.

FNAF 1

Five Nights at Freddy's is a survival horror game where you take on the role of a security guard working the night shift at a children's pizza restaurant, and must use security cameras and limited power to survive against animatronic characters that are active at night.

FNAF 2

Five Nights at Freddy's 2 (FNAF 2) is a survival horror game that is a sequel to the original Five Nights at Freddy's game. In this game, players take on the role of a security guard working the night shift at a restaurant where the animatronics have become even more dangerous and unpredictable, and must use a limited set of tools to survive against them.

Minecraft (1.5.2-1.8.8)

Minecraft is a popular sandbox game that allows players to build and explore virtual worlds made out of blocks, and to use a variety of tools and resources to create structures, mine resources, and survive against monsters that come out at night.

Slope

Slope is a fast-paced and challenging game where players control a ball as it moves down an endless slope, dodging obstacles and collecting points along the way, while also trying to reach the highest score possible.

Hole.io

Hole.io is a multiplayer online game where players control a black hole and try to swallow as many objects on the map as possible, including buildings, cars, and other players' black holes, while competing to become the biggest black hole on the map.

Retro Bowl

Retro Bowl is a mobile and PC American Football arcade game where players take control of a team, leading them to the Retro Bowl championship through a series of matches, while managing the team's strategies, signing new players, and upgrading facilities.

Paper.io

Paper.io is a multiplayer online game where players control a colored square, moving it around the map to claim as much territory as possible, while also trying to avoid running into other players' squares or the edges of the map, and competing to become the player with the most territory.

Basket Bros

Basket Bros is a mobile basketball game where players control a team of basketball players, competing against other teams in fast-paced and action-packed matches, using a variety of moves and special abilities to outsmart and outplay their opponents and become the champion.

Roblox

Roblox is a massively multiplayer online game platform that allows users to create and play games, as well as interact with other players.
